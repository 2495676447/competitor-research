### #2 — 质保范围

**Andersen 原文（复述）**:
> Q: What does the warranty cover?
> A: The warranty covers manufacturing defects in glass (20 years) and non-glass components (10 years), including material obstruction of vision, organic seal failure, hardware function, and finish degradation beyond specified limits. - 用 "organic seal failure" 而非 "broken seal" — 专业但不晦涩
